## Track 2: SME Trade Finance & Working Capital

**Goal:** Build working-capital and trade-finance solutions that reduce friction for SMEs using
stablecoin escrow, tokenized receivables, automated settlement, and verifiable payment histories.

**Prize:** 1st: 5,000 USDC / 2nd: 3,000 USDC
